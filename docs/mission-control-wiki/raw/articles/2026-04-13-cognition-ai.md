# Cognition AI

URL: https://www.cognition.ai/

Key points:
- Cognition positions itself as an Agent Lab building the future of software engineering.
- Devin is presented as an AI software engineer / collaborative AI teammate.
- The company’s focus is not broad office automation; it is agentic software engineering.
- The public product story centers on autonomous coding work, collaboration, and serious engineering teams.
- This is a useful benchmark for Hermes because it shows an agent-first product anchored to a concrete workflow domain.
