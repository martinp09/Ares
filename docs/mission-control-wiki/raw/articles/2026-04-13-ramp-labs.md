# Ramp Labs

URL: https://labs.ramp.com/

Observed themes from the homepage and product pages:
- Ramp Labs presents itself as the home for AI research at Ramp.
- The site is explicitly productized into experiments, not just blog posts.
- Public experiments include:
  - Steer AI: build and chat with activation-steered AI personalities.
  - Agent Fill: auto-fill PDF forms from a knowledge base.
  - Ramp Sheets: AI spreadsheet editor aimed at finance teams, operators, and founders.
  - AI Plays RCT: an autonomous agent managing RollerCoaster Tycoon.
  - LLM Games: model playground / benchmark-style game experiments.
- The research blog emphasizes practical AI systems, interpretability, self-maintaining workflows, and multi-agent memory sharing.
- This is a useful benchmark for Hermes because it shows a company shipping AI-first experiments, not just talking about them.
