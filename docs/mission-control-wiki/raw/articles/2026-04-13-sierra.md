# Sierra

URL: https://sierra.ai/

Key points:
- Sierra presents itself as a platform for building better, more human customer experiences with AI.
- It emphasizes one agent across multiple channels: chat, SMS, WhatsApp, email, voice, and ChatGPT.
- The product angle is enterprise customer experience, not generic chatbot tooling.
- Outcome-based pricing is part of the message.
- This is a strong reference for Hermes because it shows a production-grade, channel-aware agent platform.
