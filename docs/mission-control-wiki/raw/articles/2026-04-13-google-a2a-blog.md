# Google Developers Blog: Announcing A2A

URL: https://developers.googleblog.com/en/a2a-a-new-era-of-agent-interoperability/

Key points:
- Google frames A2A as a new era of agent interoperability.
- The blog emphasizes autonomous agents handling recurring and complex tasks.
- The design direction is not a single monolithic agent, but a network of cooperating agents.
- This supports Hermes Mission Control as an orchestration surface for multiple specialized workers.
