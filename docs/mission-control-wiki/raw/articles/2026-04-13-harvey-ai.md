# Harvey AI

URL: https://www.harvey.ai/

Key points:
- Harvey is an AI platform for legal and professional services.
- The homepage emphasizes that Harvey Agents execute legal work end-to-end.
- The platform includes Assistant, Vault, Knowledge, Workflow Agents, Mobile, and Ecosystem surfaces.
- The product story is deeply domain-specific: legal work, document analysis, drafting, research, and workflow automation.
- This is a strong benchmark for Hermes because it shows how to combine a domain-specific agent layer with a broader platform surface.
