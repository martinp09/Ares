# Microsoft AI Strategy

URL: https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/cloud-adoption-framework/ai/strategy

Key points:
- AI strategy should be grounded in business outcomes, governance, compliance, and data residency.
- Microsoft explicitly calls out standard mechanisms for AI interoperability.
- The strategy view supports choosing protocols and abstractions that reduce lock-in.
- Hermes Mission Control should optimize for interoperability, policy, and durable operations rather than a single vendor workflow.
