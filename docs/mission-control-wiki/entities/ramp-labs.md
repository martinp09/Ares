# Ramp Labs

Ramp Labs is Ramp's AI research and experiment surface. It is a good reference point for what an AI-first product org looks like when it treats agents, workflows, and operator tools as product surfaces rather than side experiments.

What stands out:
- It ships visible, named experiments.
- The experiments are practical and workflow-oriented.
- The research feels tied to operator productivity, finance workflows, and autonomous behavior.
- The public face is a mix of research blog + interactive demos.

Why Hermes should care:
- It proves that an agent-first posture can be productized.
- It supports the idea that Mission Control should include both control-plane visibility and interactive AI surfaces.
- It suggests that the future is not only chat; it is agentic tools, workflows, and operator dashboards.
