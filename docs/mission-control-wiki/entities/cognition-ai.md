# Cognition AI

Cognition AI is an agent lab focused on software engineering. Devin is the company’s collaborative AI software engineer.

Why it matters:
- It shows a strong agent-first company anchored to one high-value domain.
- It treats the agent as a teammate rather than a feature.
- It has a clear operator value proposition: accelerate engineering work.

Hermes takeaways:
- An agent-first product needs a concrete job to do.
- The product should feel like a teammate with bounded responsibility.
- Mission Control should support deploying specialists, not one giant generalist.
