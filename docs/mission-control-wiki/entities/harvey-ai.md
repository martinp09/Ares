# Harvey AI

Harvey is an AI platform for legal and professional services. Its product surface includes Assistant, Vault, Knowledge, Workflow Agents, Mobile, and Ecosystem views.

Why it matters:
- It is one of the clearest examples of a domain-specific agent platform.
- It combines research, drafting, workflow automation, and practical legal work.
- It shows how a platform can be both specialized and broad.

Hermes takeaways:
- Mission Control should support domain-specific workflows.
- Agent output should be grounded in source material and context.
- A good agent-first platform gives users multiple surfaces: assistant, knowledge, workflow, and mobile access.
