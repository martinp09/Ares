# Probate Cold Email Campaign — Professional Service Style

## Campaign name

`Email | Probate | Inherited Property Relief Plan | Texas | 2026-05`

## Campaign purpose

Cold email is the speed/testing channel. The job is not to close the seller. The job is to earn a reply that says some version of:

> Maybe. What would you need to know?

Every email should sell the mechanism/outcome, not the investor product. The mechanism is the **As-Is Inherited Property Review**.

## Core offer

## The Inherited Property Relief Plan

A simple, no-prep way for a family to find out whether an inherited property has a practical as-is path — before they repair it, clean it out, list it, host showings, get every document perfect, or solve every title/tax/heirship question first.

## Offer code to infuse everywhere

- without fixing it first
- without cleaning it out first
- without listing it publicly
- without showings
- without every document perfect
- without every family member fully aligned before the first conversation
- quick read
- simple next step
- worth discussing or not
- if keeping it is the plan, no problem
- if wrong person, I will close my notes

## Segments

### HOT probate

Use when there is verified property/tax/title/estate friction or strong operator confidence.

Angle: the property may already be turning into a project.

### WARM probate

Use when inherited-property relevance is likely but urgency/friction is less proven.

Angle: understand whether selling as-is belongs on the family’s list of options.

### COLD probate

Use when contact/property confidence is weaker or wrong-person risk is high.

Angle: wrong-person-safe property note.

- Plain text.
- No links in first touch.
- No images.
- No attachments.
- Open tracking off initially.
- Link tracking off initially.
- Stop on reply on.
- Include physical address and clean opt-out.
- Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm lead timezone.
- 3-4 active steps, then nurture.

# Reply handling

## Positive / maybe

```text
Thanks {{first_name}}. I can keep it simple.

Before I guess at anything: is the property occupied or vacant, and would the family prefer a simple as-is path over listing it publicly?
```

## Not interested

```text
Understood. I’ll stop following up.

If anything changes later and you want an as-is read for {{property_address}}, you can reach me at {{phone}}.

— {{your_name}}
```

Suppress across channels.

## Wrong person

```text
Thanks for letting me know. I’ll close my notes and remove you.
```

Suppress across channels unless they voluntarily provide the right contact.
