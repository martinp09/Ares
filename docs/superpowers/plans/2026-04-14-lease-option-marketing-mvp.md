# Lease-Option Marketing MVP Implementation Plan

> **For agentic workers:** REQUIRED: Use superpowers:subagent-driven-development (if subagents available) or superpowers:executing-plans to implement this plan. Steps use checkbox (`- [ ]`) syntax for tracking.

**Goal:** Ship a live lease-option marketing MVP where the landing page submits leads into Hermes, Hermes sends immediate SMS/email confirmations, `Cal.com` booking webhooks update booking state, non-bookers enter a 10-day SMS-intensive after 5 minutes, and inbound `TextGrid` replies drive qualification and manual-call tasks.

**Architecture:** Keep one source of truth. Hermes owns lead state, booking state, conversation state, task state, and sequence state. `Cal.com`, `TextGrid`, and `Resend` stay as provider layers. Trigger.dev handles delays, retries, and sequence timing. The landing page stops dispatching to `n8n` and instead calls Hermes directly.

**Tech Stack:** FastAPI, Pydantic, pytest, Trigger.dev, TypeScript, Next.js, Vitest, Supabase, `Cal.com`, `TextGrid`, `Resend`.
