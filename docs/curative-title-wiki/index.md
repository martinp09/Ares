## Operating Principle

Curative-title opportunities are paper-problem opportunities: owner/property situations where competition drops because ordinary buyers, wholesalers, lenders, or title companies cannot easily underwrite or insure the chain.

Keep source lanes separate from outreach readiness. A bankruptcy filing, tax delinquency, probate clue, foreclosure action, or messy title record is an evidence source — not automatic permission to market, contact, or claim legal conclusions.
