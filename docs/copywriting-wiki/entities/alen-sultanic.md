# Alen Sultanic

Alen Sultanic is the Ares copy brain's initial reference for **pain-first direct-response strategy** and copy that understands buyer psychology and the economics behind an offer.

## Ares takeaways

- Lead with the pain the seller already recognizes.
- Build agreement before presenting the offer.
- Explain the mechanism before asking for action.
- Keep copy plainspoken, specific, and emotionally accurate.
- Avoid sounding like legal advice, a threat, or a generic wholesaler blast.
