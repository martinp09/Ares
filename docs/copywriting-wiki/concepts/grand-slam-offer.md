# Grand Slam Offer

For Ares, a “grand slam offer” is not hype. It is a structured seller promise that makes the next step feel clearly worth taking while staying truthful and compliant.

## Ares offer construction checklist

1. Define the seller segment.
2. Name the painful situation in seller language.
3. Define the desired outcome.
4. Increase believable likelihood with specific capabilities.
5. Reduce time delay and effort.
6. Add de-pressure/risk-reversal language.
7. Name the offer.
8. Write channel-specific copy only after the offer is clear.

## Harris probate offer direction

Ares should offer a “simple inherited-property exit option” rather than a generic “cash offer.” The seller is not buying a cash-offer commodity; they are considering relief from repairs, cleanup, title/tax uncertainty, and family decision friction.
