# Mechanism-Based Offer

A mechanism explains why this option is different from the default path.

## Ares mechanism

Ares should frame Martin's offer as:

> A local as-is property review for inherited/probate situations where the family may not want to repair, clean out, list, or solve every title/tax issue before learning whether a buyer option exists.

This is stronger than “I buy houses cash” because it speaks to the actual friction.

## Guardrails

- Do not imply Ares provides legal, tax, or probate advice.
- Do not promise every property qualifies.
- Do not promise title/tax issues can be solved.
