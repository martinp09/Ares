# Ares Copywriting Wiki Schema

## Domain
Ares seller-facing offer and copy intelligence for Texas real-estate investing outreach, starting with Harris County probate / inherited property / possible tax-distress campaigns.

## Conventions
- File names: lowercase, hyphenated markdown.
- Every wiki page starts with YAML frontmatter.
- Use `[[wikilinks]]` between concept/entity/example pages.
- Raw sources under `raw/` are immutable capture notes; interpretations belong in wiki pages.
- Every new non-raw page must be listed in `index.md`.
- Every ingest/update must be appended to `log.md`.
- Compliance caveats stay close to offer/copy claims.

## Required Offer Lens
Every serious offer note must identify dream outcome, perceived likelihood boosters, time-delay reducers, effort/sacrifice reducers, unique mechanism, risk reversal/de-pressure language, proof gaps, and compliance risk.

## Required Copy Lens
Every serious copy note must identify awareness level, pain-first hook, belief shift or failed-solution contrast, mechanism, offer/CTA, channel fit, and truth/risk note.
