# Probate HOT Offer Examples

## Offer one-liner

> If the inherited property is becoming a project the family does not want, I can review it as-is and let you know whether a simple buyer option makes sense before you repair, clean out, list, or untangle every issue first.

## HOT email offer paragraph

> Situations like this can get complicated when repairs, cleanup, taxes, title questions, or multiple family members are involved. A normal listing is not always the easiest first step. I buy some Harris County properties as-is and can review this one to see whether a simple option makes sense.

## Why it works

- Names real probate friction.
- Uses “some” and “whether” to avoid guarantee language.
- Offers a small next step instead of a hard close.
