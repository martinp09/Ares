# SMS Permission Openers

## HOT permission opener

> Hi {{first_name}}, this is Martin. I’m reaching out about {{property_address}}. If the family is considering selling it as-is, is it okay if I ask one quick question?

## WARM permission opener

> Hi {{first_name}}, Martin here. Are you the right person to ask about the Harris County property at {{property_address}}?

## Wrong person reply

> Thanks for letting me know. I’ll remove this number from my notes.

## Compliance stance

- Do not blast COLD/REST.
- No links in first SMS.
- Identify Martin by name.
- Use suppression/DNC review before sends.
